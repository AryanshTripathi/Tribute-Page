Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose

The man who gave India freedom

Here is the timeline of Netaji

  • 1919: He passed the ICS exam but turned down the offer because he had to work under the British
  • 1919: He left India for Great Britain
  • 1923: He was elected leader of the All India Youth Council and Secretary of Bengal State Congress
  • 1925: Bose was arrested by the British Government
  • 1927: He got released from prison and started working with Nehru
  • 1930: Bose became Mayor of Calcutta
  • 1938: Bose accepted nomination as the INC presidential candidate
  • 1939: He organised the All India Forward Bloc
  • 1941: Bose escaped from India after it becam hostile there for him
  • 1945: Subhash Chandra Bose disappeared
  • He was said to die in a plan crash on the way to Taiwan, but this is hotly disputed and never been proved

Some Inspirational Quotes by Netaji

  • Freedom is not given, it's taken
  • No real change is history is ever achieved by discussions
  • Give me blood, and I will give you freedom
  • We should have but one desire today - the desire to die so that India may live - the desire to face a martyr's death, so that the path to freedom may be paved with the martyr's blood.
